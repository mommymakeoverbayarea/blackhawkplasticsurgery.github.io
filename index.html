Surgery Risks Overview

Taking into account the potential risks and complications is vital before undergoing a Mommy Makeover surgery in the Bay Area. It's important to prioritize safety and well-being throughout the entire process. Here are some key points to bear in mind:
  • Infection: Risk of infection is present in any surgical procedure.
  • Anesthesia Risks: Adverse reactions to anesthesia can occur, though rare.
  • Scarring: While efforts are made to minimize scarring, individual healing may vary.
  • Blood Clots: Surgery increases the risk of blood clots forming in the legs.
  • Sensation Changes: Temporary or permanent changes in breast or abdominal sensation can occur post-surgery.
Prioritizing these considerations can help ensure a safer Mommy Makeover experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Undergo a Mommy Makeover if I Plan to Have More Children?

We recommend waiting until you're done having children before considering a mommy makeover. This procedure is designed to address changes from pregnancy, and future pregnancies may impact results. Prioritize your safety and best possible outcome.

How Soon After Childbirth Can I Schedule a Mommy Makeover?

After childbirth, we recommend waiting at least six months before considering a mommy makeover to give your body sufficient time to heal. Consult with a board-certified plastic surgeon for personalized guidance and the best timing.

Are There Non-Surgical Alternatives to a Mommy Makeover?

Yes, there are non-surgical alternatives to a mommy makeover. These options may include diet, exercise, and non-invasive treatments. Consulting with a medical professional is essential to determine the best approach for individual needs.

Will Insurance Cover Any Part of the Mommy Makeover Procedure?

Insurance typically does not cover mommy makeover procedures as they are considered elective cosmetic surgeries. It's important to review your policy and discuss payment options with your healthcare provider to guarantee proper financial planning.

What Post-Operative Support Is Available for Mommy Makeover Patients?

After your mommy makeover, we provide thorough post-operative support. Our team guarantees you have clear instructions, follow-up appointments, and access to any necessary resources. Your well-being is our top priority.
